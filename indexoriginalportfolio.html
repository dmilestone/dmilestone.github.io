Hello, world! Danielle has enjoyed a successful twelve year sales career in the Atlanta area and in 2019 she decided it was time to broaden her skill set and challenge her more analytical side.

She enrolled in the Georgia Tech Data Science Bootcamp that summer began riding the highs and lows of writing code that works. Much like sales, writing code is full of ups and downs, never boring, and problems are constantly changing so she felt right at home.

Please enjoy her professional portfolio which she had a blast making for you and feel free to reach out to her if you have any questions or maybe a lead on her next big adventure.
